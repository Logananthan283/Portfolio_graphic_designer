About Me

I’m a detail-oriented Graphic & UI Designer who blends minimal aesthetics with user-focused interaction design. My work is driven by clear hierarchy, refined typography, and thoughtful layouts that scale seamlessly across devices.

Key achievements and experience:

  • Created 50+ Instagram post designs that maintain consistent branding, clean composition, and strong storytelling through visual hierarchy.
  • Executed 5+ end-to-end UI/UX projects in Figma — covering research, wireframing, component creation, UI kits, and high-fidelity prototypes.
  • Developed and optimized a Shopify store with UX-driven improvements, focusing on navigation clarity, product layout structure, mobile responsiveness, and conversion-friendly design.
  • Hands-on experience in eCommerce workflows — product page layouts, collection structuring, PDP/PLP improvement, and theme customization for better user flow.
  • Performed manual testing on 5+ websites, identifying UI inconsistencies, interaction issues, browser-compatibility problems, and accessibility gaps to refine overall user experience.

I prioritize clean spacing, consistent alignment, and cohesive system thinking. Whether designing a social asset, an interface, or an eCommerce layout, my goal is to create experiences that are visually minimal, structurally clear, and easy for users to navigate.

Tools & Strengths

  • Graphic Design: Canva, Affinity Designer — brand visuals, marketing assets, and scalable layouts.
  • UI/UX in Figma: Design systems, UI kits, component libraries, prototype flows, and developer-ready handoff.
  • Typography & Color Systems: Type hierarchy, modular scaling, accessible contrast, and cohesive color palettes.
  • Responsive Layouts: Grid systems, adaptive spacing, mobile-first thinking, and component behavior across breakpoints.
  • Shopify & eCommerce: Theme customization, PDP & PLP layout optimization, UX improvements, navigation flow, and conversion-focused design decisions.
  • Manual Testing & QA: Cross-browser testing, usability checks, visual bug detection, interaction validation, and reporting actionable improvements.
